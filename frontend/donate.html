How to donate breast milk

Most donation journeys are simple and guided. Milk banks usually help you through each stage clearly.

  • Contact a nearby human milk bank or hospital program that accepts donor milk.
  • Complete a short health and lifestyle screening questionnaire.
  • Finish any basic approval steps the milk bank requires.
  • Collect milk in sterilized containers and label it carefully.
  • Keep it frozen until pickup, drop-off, or the bank’s next instruction.
