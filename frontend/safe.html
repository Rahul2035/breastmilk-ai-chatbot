Safety first

Is breast milk donation safe?

Yes, registered milk banks use screening and handling processes designed to protect both donors and babies.

  • Donors are usually screened with health and lifestyle questions before approval.
  • Collected milk is handled using strict storage and hygiene standards.
  • Milk banks often pasteurize donor milk to reduce harmful bacteria and risks.
  • Hospitals and NICUs rely on donor milk as a trusted support option for vulnerable babies.
  • Following storage and labeling guidance helps keep the donation process safe and smooth.
